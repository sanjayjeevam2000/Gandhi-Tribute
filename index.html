Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi (1869-1948) was a leader of India's independence movement and a pioneer of nonviolent resistance. His principles of truth and nonviolence inspired many movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Gandhi played a crucial role in India's freedom struggle against British rule through movements like the Non-Cooperation Movement, Civil Disobedience Movement, and Quit India Movement.
